## Summary
> The Ontario Reign cap off their seven-game homestand Wednesday night when they host the Henderson Silver Knights at Toyota Arena for the fourth meeting of the season between the two Pacific Division clubs. Ontario has won two of their first three matchups against the Silver Knights, winning 5-3 on Oct. 18 before splitting a home-and-home weekend three weeks ago by losing 3-1 in the opener and bouncing back for a 3-2 victory the following afternoon. The Reign are in possession of third place in the Pacific coming into Wednesday’s contest with 25 points, while Henderson sits in ninth with 13 points accrued.
